Seaport District Sea Sculptures

Leta Sobierajski, an artist/designer based in New York City has works which is incredibly diverse, ranging from conventional identities to visually appealing bizarre composition. Leta studied Graphic Design at Purchase Collage and has been operating since 2013, since then she has been recognized as an art director as well being Print magazine's new Visual Artist. Sobierajski is also known to give talks all aover the world for instance in Europe, North America and Asia
